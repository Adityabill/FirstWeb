Accomodation in the City

There are a lots of hotels ranging from 5 star to 1-star and also homestay residencies. Lets have a look into some of these places:

5-Star Hotels

  • ITC Royal Bengal:

    • It is located at EM Bypass near Maa Flyover. It is 15.3 Km from Kolkata Airport. Popular attraction "Science City" are located just at a walkable distance. Other attractions like Victoria Memorial, Nicco Park are located 7 km and 5 km respectively. Click Here To Book

  • ITC Sonar Bangla:

    • It is located at EM Bypass besides ITC Royal Bengal.Click Here To Book.

  • The Grand Oberoi Hotel(also called The Grand Hotel):

    • It is located in the central business district of Esplanade at Jawaharlal Nehru Road(formerly Chowringhee Road). The famous New Market and Victoria Memorial are located at close vicinity from the hotel. The hotel is an elegant buliding of Colonial British Era.Click Here To Book

  • The Lalit Great Eastern Hotel:

    • It is located in the central business district of B.B.D Bagh (formerly Dalhousie Square). It is one of the oldest hotel in India which was opened in 1841, when Kolkata (formerly Calcutta) was the Capital Of Colonial India.Click Here To Book

  • Taj Bengal:

    • It is located in the upscale neighbourhood of Alipore. Popular landmarks like Victoria Memorial, Alipore Zoological Garden, Royal Calcutta Race Course are in close proximity from the hotel.Click Here To Book

  • The Park Hotel:

    • It located in the upscale street of Park Street. Victoria Memorial and New Market.Click Here To Book

  • Novotel:

    • It is located in the IT and business hub of Rajarhat Newtown. The hotel is just an 11 km ride from the airport. Malls like Axis Mall and City Centre-II are located at close proximity.Click Here To Book

  • Hotel Hindustan International:

    • It is located at 235, 1, A.J.C Bose Road, Sreepally, Elgin. The hotel is 2Km away from Victoria Memorial and 3 Km from Alipore Zoo.Click Here To Book

And the list goes on and on......
